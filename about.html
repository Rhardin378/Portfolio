About

I'm a full-stack software engineer with a passion for developing powerful web applications. My journey into software engineering started with working as a mental health case manager. Daily, I had to utilize an electronic health records system that was not very user-friendly. While utilizing this record system I developed a deep interest in making more user-friendly web applications. In my free time, I began learning software development and building full-stack web applications. I found my passion working in development and plan to join a team of developers to be able to work collaboratively to provide a positive impact on a larger scale.

I currently have experience working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript . I also have experience working with Node, React as well as Tailwind CSS and BootStrap. I am continuously learning and improving my skills on a daily basis and look forward to becoming an asset to the development team at my next opportunity.
